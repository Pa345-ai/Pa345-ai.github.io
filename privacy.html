Privacy Framework

Global Data Protection & Compliance Standard (v2025.1)

Ranasinghe Infrastructure System operates at the intersection of high-scale automation and absolute data integrity. This framework governs how we handle "Information Assets" for our global enterprise partners.

1. Merchant of Record & Financial Governance

Our global order processing is managed by Paddle.com Market Ltd. As our Merchant of Record, Paddle is the legal entity responsible for the transaction. Ranasinghe Infrastructure System never comes into contact with, stores, or processes your full credit card number, CVV, or banking credentials.

Paddle is fully PCI-DSS Level 1 Compliant and adheres to the SOC2 security framework, ensuring your $10,000+ transactions are shielded by banking-grade encryption.

2. Detailed Data Classification

A. Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We collect only what is strictly necessary to grant license access: Full Legal Name, Corporate Email Address, and Billing Address.

B. Transactional Artifacts

For the $10,000 Full IP Buyout, we record the timestamp, transaction ID, and the specific version of the Infrastructure Kit delivered. This is required for legal proof of ownership transfer.

C. Attribution Data

We use unique Broker Attribution IDs via our affiliate system (Endorsely). This records the referral source but does not link your identity to any third-party marketing databases.

3. Data Retention & Destruction Policy

We do not hold your data indefinitely. Our retention schedule is as follows:

  • Marketing Data: Deleted after 12 months of inactivity.
  • License Records: Retained for the duration of your active license to provide security patches.
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by international tax law (Managed by Paddle).

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA / APPI)

Regardless of your location, we extend the following rights to all customers:

  • Right to Erasure: You may request the "Right to be Forgotten" once your license expires.
  • Right to Portability: You may request a copy of all data we hold on your organization in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may opt-out of all non-essential communications at any time.

5. Information Security Measures

Access to customer license data is restricted to "Need-to-Know" personnel. All internal access requires Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and is logged for auditing purposes. In the event of a data breach, all affected parties will be notified within 72 hours.
